Health Tips for Today

💧 Hydration

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Dehydration worsens headaches, fatigue, and common symptoms.

🥦 Nutrition

Include dal, sabzi, and seasonal vegetables. These provide micronutrients that support your immune system.

🚶 Movement

A 30-minute brisk walk daily significantly reduces cardiovascular risk and improves energy and mood.

❤️ Heart Attack Warning Signs

Chest pressure or squeezing · Pain radiating to arm, jaw, or back · Shortness of breath · Cold sweats · Nausea · Lightheadedness

🩸 Severe Bleeding — First Aid
