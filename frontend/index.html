📖 Normal Range Reference

Blood Pressure

Normal: <120/80 mmHg
Elevated: 120–129/<80
High: ≥130/80 mmHg

Blood Glucose

Fasting normal: 70–99 mg/dL
Pre-diabetic: 100–125
Diabetic: ≥126 mg/dL

Temperature

Normal: 97–99°F
Low-grade fever: 99–100.4°F
Fever: >100.4°F

SpO₂ (Oxygen)

Normal: 95–100%
Concern: 90–94%
Emergency: <90%
